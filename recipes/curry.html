Red Curry

Description

Reduce skillet heat to simmer. Add the noodles and let simmer until flavors are absorbed, about 5 minutes. Divide chicken and noodles among individual serving bowls.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. coconut oil
  • 16 oz. skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into small cubes
  • 14 oz. cream of coconut
  • 11oz red Thai curry sauce
  • 16 oz. dried rice stick vermicelli noodles

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large skillet on high heat. Add chicken cubes; cook until browned (about 2 minutes per side). Reduce heat to medium-high and add coconut cream and curry sauce. Cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear (about 5 minutes). An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F.
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil; stir in vermicelli pasta and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is tender yet firm to the bite, 4 to 5 minutes. Drain.
  3. Reduce skillet heat to simmer. Add the noodles and let simmer until flavors are absorbed, about 5 minutes. Divide chicken and noodles among individual serving bowls.
